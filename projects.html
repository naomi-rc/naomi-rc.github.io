iTutorU

December 10th, 2018

iTutorU is an inter-tutoring platform designed for CEGEP students. Students are able to create and account, sign in, and search for tutors in subjects they need help in or subjects of interest to them. Tutors can be searched by program, subjects they tutor, and cost. iTutorU includes a messaging system for contact between students and tutors, a store of resources categorized by program, and the ability to jot down notes.
